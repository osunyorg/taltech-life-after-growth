Ragnar Nurkse Department of Innovation and Governance is an internationally recognized interdisciplinary research center at TalTech School of Business and Governance. Our top researchers from Estonia and the rest of the world bring together the best international and local knowledge in the fields of global governance, public administration, technology and e-governance, fiscal and innovation policies & philosophy and ethics. With our five forward-looking and practical study programs, we are the only institution in Estonia to offer education in innovation and public administration at BA, MA and PhD levels. Our research, study and innovation activities are closely intertwined with public partnership, international cooperation and social dialogue.
