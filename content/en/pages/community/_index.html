Crafting shared imaginaries and practices of Life after growth 

body: >-


Life after growth aims to evolve to an open space for research, sharing, and experimentation around post-growth pathways of collective action and organising. The idea of Life after growth has been initiated in the first Summer School in 2023 and has since been looking for new domains of collective action to spill over. This page aspires to evolve into a collective space to co-create a common narrative for a flourishing community of researchers, practitioners, activists, and enthusiasts, by gathering stories, experiences, and imaginaries of life after growth, situated in the here and now.
