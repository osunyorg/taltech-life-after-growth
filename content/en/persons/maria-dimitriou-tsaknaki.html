Maria Dimitriou-Tsaknaki (she, her) is a Ph.D candidate in the field of critical technology theory and the commons, based in Thessaloniki, Greece. With a background in architecture, her interests are related to environmental & participatory design, circular economy practices (focusing on participatory material reuse), commoning practices, community building, ecological justice and landscape democracy. Her research aims to blend theoretical investigations with practice-based experiences, while exploring alternative technical systems and modes of technological production as models for commoning knowledge. Her worlds can be found here (external link) (Distributed Design), here (external link) (degrowth.info (external link)) and here (external link) (SMOOTH).
