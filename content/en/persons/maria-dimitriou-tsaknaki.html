Maria Dimitriou-Tsaknaki (she, her) is living in Thessaloniki, Greece and she is a graduate of School of Architecture, AUTh. Her interests are related to theories of technology, alternative systems of knowledge, exploring the place between theory and practice, academia and social sphere. She has worked as an architect in Greece and for a short time in France where she was engaged in research on the participatory material reuse as an educational process of knowledge sharing and community building.
