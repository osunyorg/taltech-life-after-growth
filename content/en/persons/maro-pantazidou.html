Maro works at the intersection of participatory strategy development, radical education and collaborative research. She has served as the deputy director of global strategy and impact for Amnesty International and has collaborated with bigger and smaller organisations and networks who wish to reflect on their approach to social change. As a facilitator and/or researcher she has been part of transnational research projects (University of Sussex, University of York, LSE and others) that focus on civic action and socio-political transitions. Her current research centres around the future of work and its links to socio-ecological transformation.
