Ben Robra is a lecturer at the Business School of the University of Queensland, Australia. He's also a fellow at the P2P Lab and a founding editor of the Degrowth Journal. Ben's research focuses on degrowth and post-growth in connection to organisation, commoning, and technology. His interests are particularly on alternative modes of production and organisation and how they might help to achieve a sustainable society.
